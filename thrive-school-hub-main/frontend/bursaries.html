Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible to apply for bursaries?
Ugandan students enrolled in recognized educational institutions from Kindergarten to University level are eligible. Specific requirements vary by bursary type, including academic performance and financial need.
What documents do I need to apply?
You'll need: Birth Certificate or ID, latest academic report cards, recommendation letter from your school, passport-sized photo, and a written statement of need. Additional documents may be requested based on your situation.
How long does the application process take?
Applications are reviewed within 2-3 weeks. Shortlisted candidates may be called for an interview. Final decisions are communicated via email and SMS within 30 days of application deadline.
Can I apply for multiple bursaries?
You may only apply for one bursary per academic year. Choose the one that best matches your needs and qualifications. Multiple applications will be automatically disqualified.
How is the bursary money disbursed?
Funds are disbursed directly to the educational institution on behalf of the student, ensuring they are used for tuition and approved educational expenses. Book allowances are provided as vouchers.

Need Urgent Assistance?

Our bursary support team is here to help. Contact us for any questions or assistance with your application.
