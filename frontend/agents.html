Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an agent?
Fill out the registration form above with your details. Once submitted, our team will review your application within 2-3 business days. Approved agents receive a confirmation email with login credentials.
How are commissions calculated?
Commissions are based on a tier system: 5% for 0-10 referrals, 7% for 11-25 referrals, 10% for 26-50 referrals, and 12% for 51+ referrals. Commission is calculated based on tuition fees paid by referred students.
When do I get paid?
Commissions are paid monthly on the 15th. Payments are made via MTN Mobile Money or bank transfer. You need a minimum of UGX 80,000 to receive payment.
Can I track my referrals?
Yes! Once registered, you'll have access to a personalized dashboard where you can track all referrals, commissions, and performance metrics in real-time.
What schools can I refer students to?
Our network includes over 85 partner schools across all levels - from Kindergarten to University, including technical institutes and Tahfidh schools. You can refer students to any of our verified partner institutions.
